Ground operation:
  • Keep column back
  • Upper cowls: open
  • Lower cowls: open

Take-off:
  1. Brakes: on
  2. Column: neutral
  3. Upper cowls: closed
  4. Lower cowls: open
  5. Flaps: 50%
  6. Throttle: Increase softly to maximum
  7. When manifold pressure reaches 30"Hg, release brakes
  8. When speed reaches 110mph, column back
  9. Landing gear: up
  10. Level flight, not climb
  11. When speed reaches 135mph, flaps up
  12. Adjust throttle and RPM
